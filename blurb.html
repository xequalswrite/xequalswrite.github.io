STEM

science. technology. engineering. math. the nitty gritty details and rules of life and how we can improve them.

art

the expression of thoughts, feelings, and questions on paper and canvas and film. your imagination brought to real life.

so what happens when we cross STEM + art and put it on paper? we get this: x = write, a collection of science and math and stories all rolled into one.

if you've ever written a sonnet and thought to compare sibling separation to cytokinesis, this magazine is for you.

be a artist and an engineer. bring your favorite nucleotides, integrals, and data structures into your prose and poetry--and discover others who do the same.

welcome to x = write.
